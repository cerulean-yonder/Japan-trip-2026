## Transport Notes

- **IC Card (Suica/Pasmo):** Get one at Narita on arrival. Works on almost all trains, buses, and at convenience stores nationwide.
- **Shinkansen route:** Kamakura → Tokyo (Yokosuka Line) → Echigo Yuzawa/Nagaoka (Joetsu Shinkansen) → Niigata (Joetsu Shinkansen) → Tokyo (Joetsu Shinkansen)
- **Book Shinkansen seats in advance** — late April/early May is Japan's **Golden Week**, the busiest domestic travel period of the year. Trains sell out.
- **JR Pass:** Consider whether the cost is worth it based on total Shinkansen use — Narita→Kamakura + Tokyo↔Niigata (×2) + Tokyo→Narita may make it worthwhile.
