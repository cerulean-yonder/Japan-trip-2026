🚅 Transport Notes
IC Card (Suica / Pasmo) Get one at Narita on arrival. Works on almost all trains, buses, and at convenience stores nationwide.
Shinkansen Route Kamakura → Tokyo (Yokosuka Line) → Echigo Yuzawa / Nagaoka (Joetsu Shinkansen) → Niigata → Tokyo (Joetsu Shinkansen)
Book Shinkansen seats in advance ⚠️ Late April / early May is Japan's Golden Week — the busiest domestic travel period of the year. Trains sell out.
